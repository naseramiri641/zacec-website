About ZACEC

ZACEC is a locally registered construction and engineering firm with experience in donor-funded and government projects. Our team includes civil engineers, site supervisors, and skilled labor capable of delivering turnkey solutions while following safety, quality, and environmental guidelines.

Services

  • Civil Construction & Rehabilitation — Buildings, health centers, classrooms, and site development.
  • WASH Systems — Water supply, storage tanks, sanitation blocks, plumbing and drainage.
  • Retaining & Boundary Walls — Design and construction in hilly and unstable terrains.
  • Solar Water Systems — Solar pumps, elevated tanks, and solar integration for remote sites.
  • Project Management — Planning, BOQ, procurement, supervision and quality control.
  • Access & Site Works — Access paths, ramps, landscaping and security fencing.
